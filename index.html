About the App

One App for All Your Studies

Vidya Topper is a free mobile app made specifically for Indian students. Whether you're in Class 6 or preparing for a government entrance exam, the app gives you everything you need — in one simple, clean interface.

Study NCERT chapters, practice with quizzes, create flashcards, write notes, plan your schedule, and track your progress — all without spending a single rupee. The app also works offline so there's no barrier to learning.

No complicated features, no subscriptions — just focused learning tools designed for students who want to study smart.

Board Exam Papers

Government Exam Content

Flashcards

How It Works

Start Studying in 4 Simple Steps

No tutorials, no complexity — just download and start studying.

⬇️

Download & Sign Up

Install the app for free from the app store and create your account with email or phone in under a minute.

📂

Choose Your Category

Pick your exam type — NCERT, Board Exams, or Government Exams. Then select your class or subject.

📖

Study & Practice

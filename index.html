About Vidya Topper

Your Complete Study Partner

Vidya Topper is a free mobile app built for students who want a simple, organised way to study. Whether you're preparing for your Board exams, NCERT syllabus, or a government entrance exam — everything you need is here.

The app is designed to be lightweight, easy to use, and works even when internet is limited. No unnecessary clutter — just your study material, practice tests, and tools that actually help.

Features

Everything You Need to Prepare

Simple, useful tools that cover the full preparation journey — from reading to practicing to tracking your progress.

📚

NCERT Study Material

Access organised NCERT chapter content for multiple classes and subjects — structured for easy reading and revision.

🏫

Board Exam Preparation

Previous year question papers and chapter-wise content aligned to CBSE and state board syllabi.

🏛️

Government Exam Content

Study material organised for students preparing for common government competitive entrance exams.

✏️

Practice Quizzes

Take chapter-wise quizzes to test what you've studied. Get instant feedback so you know what to revisit.

🃏

Flashcards

Quick-revision flashcards to help you memorise key facts, formulas, and concepts before exams.

📝

Notes

Create and save your own study notes inside the app. Keep everything in one organised place.

📌

Bookmarks

Save important chapters, questions, or content to your bookmarks for quick access anytime.

📅

Study Planner

Plan your study sessions day by day. Set goals, track what you've covered, and stay consistent.

Study Timer

Use a built-in timer to focus during your study sessions. Track how long you study each day.

Download content and study without internet. Useful for students with limited data or connectivity.

Support Chat

Student Profile

Track your personal progress, view your study stats, and keep up with your preparation journey.

How It Works

Getting Started Is Easy

Follow these simple steps and start studying in minutes.

1

Download & Sign Up

Install the app for free from the app store. Create your account with your email or phone number in seconds.

2

Choose Your Exam or Subject

Select what you're studying — NCERT chapters, Board exam papers, or Government exam content. The app organises everything clearly.

3

Study, Practice & Revise

Read content, take quizzes, use flashcards, and make notes. Use the study timer to stay focused during sessions.

4

Plan & Track Progress

Use the study planner to set daily targets. Check your profile to see your progress and consistency over time.
